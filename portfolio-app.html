Core Features

What it does.

Every feature is designed around a single goal: turning raw market data into actionable intelligence.

Predictive ML Models
Custom algorithms achieving 65%+ accuracy on long-term time-series forecasting, with a modular pipeline enabling rapid iteration and retraining.
AI Score Summary
Five-dimensional scoring system — News Sentiment, Fundamentals, Financials, Valuation, and Technicals — providing a holistic view of each stock.
Portfolio Analyst
AI-driven analysis of portfolio composition with sector exposure breakdown, diversification suggestions, and concrete rebalancing proposals.
Risk Management
Configurable risk tolerance levels with dynamic allocation monitoring. Identifies overexposure, concentration risk, and suggests defensive positions.
Real-time Charts
Interactive candlestick and line charts with switchable views. Visual analytics dashboards for monitoring risk, allocation, and performance trends.
News & Watchlist
Curated market news feed with sentiment analysis integration. Personalized watchlist to track and monitor stocks of interest.
AI Intelligence

Multi-dimensional scoring.

Each stock is evaluated across five independent AI models, giving you a comprehensive view beyond any single metric.
